# regencyrecovery.com URL Map

## Site model (read first)

Regency Recovery & Wellness Center is a **licensed non-profit addiction treatment and mental health facility** physically located in **Prescott, Arizona**. It is NOT a referral or placement network — it owns and operates its own programs and admits clients directly. Active voice: "we treat," "our facility," "our team," "our programs," "our Prescott center," "available 24/7."

The primary conversion goal: drive phone calls to **(928) 460-7001**. The secondary CTA is the contact form (/contact-us/); the tertiary/mission CTA is /donate/ (it is a non-profit). Canonical model page for clinical depth and furniture: the homepage + program pillar pages.

## Hard rules for every page (protects rankings + the call)

- Regency is a real facility, so geo and ownership language is legitimate: "our Prescott facility," "we admit," "our clinical team," "our residential program" are all allowed and encouraged. This is the opposite of a referral-network disclaimer — lean into being the actual provider.
- Never guarantee treatment outcomes, sobriety, "cure," coverage outcomes, in-network status, or specific out-of-pocket figures. Coverage = "we verify your benefits before admission"; outcomes = framed as evidence-based, individualized, never promised.
- CTA language: "Call (928) 460-7001," "Contact our admissions team," "Verify your benefits," "Begin your recovery," "Reach our 24/7 helpline." Keep the urgency without false guarantees.
- Every page touching overdose, detox danger, or severe withdrawal shows: call 911 for medical emergency; call/text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline; plus Arizona crisis options. Crisis blockquote near top AND at close on any clinical-risk page.
- Non-profit framing is a differentiator vs. for-profit Prescott competitors — reference the mission/home-like intimate setting where natural, but don't let it dilute the clinical authority.
- Match the homepage/program pages for furniture: clear H1, scannable program list, "Why Choose Regency" trust block, testimonial/social proof, mid-page + footer call CTA, and the 24/7 helpline number.

## CTA modes

- **call-primary**: phone CTA above the fold + repeated; contact form secondary. (program, substance, emergency-intent, geo)
- **education-primary**: trust/education leads, call CTA mid + end. (education, comparison, blog, top-of-funnel)
- Both always include **(928) 460-7001** and a 24/7 helpline reference.

## How to use this map (instruction for the writing skill)

When given a slug or topic:

1. Find its row in the tracker below (or the closest cluster if it's a new topic).
2. Use **page type** to select the article template/structure.
3. Use **parent/pillar + cluster** to build internal links — always link up to the parent pillar, sideways to 2–3 cluster siblings, and to /contact-us/ + the relevant crisis/detox page.
4. Use **CTA mode** to set the conversion action (always the call to (928) 460-7001).
5. Use **target keyword** as the primary; add natural variations.
6. FIND A URL THAT HASN'T BEEN BUILT/REFRESHED and after building or rewriting a page mark the status as ✅.
